🎉 Party Planner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

Party Planner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Username and email address when you create an account
  • Event data: Event details, guest lists, menus, budgets, and timelines you create
  • Guest information: Names, email addresses, and RSVP responses of guests you add
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Party Planner service
  • To send event invitations to guests on your behalf via email
  • To generate AI-powered event planning suggestions using AWS Bedrock
  • To authenticate your account securely via AWS Cognito
  • To improve and personalize your experience

3. AI Features

Party Planner uses AWS Bedrock (Claude AI) to generate event planning suggestions, menus, timelines, and invitation content. Your event details are sent to AWS to generate these suggestions. We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): For hosting, authentication, AI features, and email delivery
  • Your guests: When you send invitations, guest names and event details are included

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on AWS servers in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption and AWS Cognito for authentication. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or export your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@mim-online.com

7. Children's Privacy

Party Planner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
